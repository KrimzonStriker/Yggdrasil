Langue de Camazotz dyed in three colors

A twist to make the Camazotz bats of Svartalfheim more freshing. Served in three forms, starting with a fresh citrius liver wrap, crispy langue with clear noodles, and a thin breast slice on tomato puree.

Braised Herot Stir Fry.

This dish comes from a white stag of the Alfheim forests, whose hunting permits were negotiated with that realms native inhabitants. Paired with a thick red wine and sweet chestnut sauce, fried mandrake roots, and rice noodles, this dish is designed to bring out the rich sweetness of the venison.

Pancetta and Basil Wrapped Cockatrice.

Combined with a glazed date sauce for additional acidity the pancetta and basil wrap works will the sumptious and spicy roasted free range cocaktrice breast, courtesy of the Vanir home of Vanaheim.

Braised Dragon Steak.

Braised in a thick and rich stew for several hours, the legendary toughness of the Jotunheim dragons has been tenderized to its peak and only accentuated by the honey glaze sauce.

Niflheim Chocolate Lava Cake.

Forged in its fiery ovens and famous cast iron caludrons the Nilfheim Chocolate Lave Cake is the pinnacle of absolute temparture control. Baked and melted to near perfection this mouth watering decadent luxury is the perfect cap off after a thrilling adventure across nine worlds.
