Enter the Nine Relams of Fine Dining

Open the doors to Yggdrisal and discover a one of a kind establishment that serves only the finest cuisine from all across the legends of Norse Mythology. Try sumptious melted baked goods from the ovens of Muspelheim or perhaps sample a refresing cold desert carved from the icey mountains of Niflheim instead. With the food of nine worlds available the amount of flavors to be tasted are limited only by your imagination.
